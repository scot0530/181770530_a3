Our History

We were founded in the late 1700s by Swedish explorers who were searching for what some believe to be just a fairy tale: The Legendary Golden Donut. Bards have been known to sing songs and fables about this mysterious yet delicious tale, however none were able to discover whether the tales were true. Emperor Warrior-King Joseph Scott, descendant of King Arthur and Genghis Khan decided to take action to discover the truth. He, and his loyal servants embarked on a legendary tale of myth to build Holy Donuts from The Golden Donut!

Beyond Dough

We are dedicated to keeping all of our donuts free from artifical flavouring and ensure that they are sourced from sustainable sources from our many partners in countries such as Canada, The United States of America, Mexico, Belgium, Germany, The United Kingdom, France, Switzerland, and Mongolia! Together we work on making the donut world as sustainable as possible by not only reducing emissions to zero, but also by having a positive emissions impact through our donut tree planting charities.
